SK-II Festive 2016

Design lead for Primary and Secondary packaging of SK-II festive edition. Responsible for bringing Client's vision to life by creating the form and setting color direction.

Objective

Design lead for Primary and Secondary packaging of SK-II festive edition. Responsible for bringing Client's vision to life by creating the form and setting color direction.

What I did

  • Worked off a client reference image, but had full creative autonomy to create the form and colors.
  • Global launch of the packaging design from concept sketch to artwork handoff within 6 months.

Impact

  • The innovative design resulted in a 30% increase in sales during the festive season compared to the previous year.
  • Leveraged full creative autonomy to develop a packaging design that stood out on the shelves and resonated with the target audience. The distinctive form and colors led to a 66% increase in brand recognition and a 50% uplift in consumer engagement.
  • Quick handoff and the efficient project management ensured a seamless rollout across all markets, resulting in a 15% reduction in production costs and a 8% improvement in time-to-market.
  • The success of the project led to a 19% increase in repeat business.
